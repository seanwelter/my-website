Silverdale Area Walks

Silverdale Waterfront

There's a lot to see and do on this walk - I generally start by walking along the length of the beach, and then stopping by some of the smaller shops near the Yacht Club.

Clear Creek Trail

You can tie this walk into the waterfront walk, as this trail winds through the entirety of Silverdale. I start this at the Old Mill Park, and usually end up behind St Michael's by the end.

Gateway Park

If you have a dog, there's a dog park. If you're a skater, there's a skate park. If you want to walk through a marsh, there's a nice wooden bridge that goes right through the middle of it with some amazing sights.

Island Lake

Walk along the shores of a lake with an island in it and marvel at both the beauty of nature, and our naming prowess. You can cut into the woods along the path and make your way up to the top of the hill, but you might have trouble seeing anything as the trees get thicker the further up you go, somehow.
