Other Area Walks

Elanden Gardens

You can still walk along the waterfront here, but the last time I went here it was closed to the public. You get a gorgeous view of the entire bay area though, so still worth the trip.

Poulsbo Waterfront

This place is a Viking experience nestled along the waters north of Silverdale. Try the bear claws!

Bainbridge Japanese Memorial

A bit of a drive, but well worth it, this is a memorial to all the Japanese people who were moved around during World War II to the internment camps. It's well cared for and offers an amazing view.

Keyport Waterfront

The last time I was here it was very hard to see anything due to all the rain and fog, but I have been told by many people that I missed out on a great vista at the very tip of Keyport.
