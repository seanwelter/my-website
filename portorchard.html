Port Orchard Area Walks

Waterfront South

Starting from the Marina and ending just past the Hi Tide Bar and Grill, this is a short and sweet walk that you can make longer by detouring down Bay Street to explore all the neat little shops along the main street.

Waterfront North

A longer walk that can feel a little more desolate on rainier days, this walk along the shorefont up to the Annapolis Foot Ferry terminal is breathtaking. Watch out for traffic, though!

Regional Parks

Manette has a lot of smaller parks that you can string together. My preferred route starts at Veterans Memorial Park, then south to the Regional Park, and finishing up by doing a circuit around Madrona Drive.

Blackjack Creek

If you're brave, you can hike along this creek for quite a while. It's not well maintained, and occasionally you will have to detour around roads, but it's a beautiful hike.
